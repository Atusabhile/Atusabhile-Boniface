CONVOCATION THEME

Modernising data ecosytem to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area(AfCFTA):
The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development in Tanzania.

Overview of the 6th Convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC)

The graduation convocation marks a significant milestone in the academic journey of our students. It is a time to recognize their hard work, dedication, and achievements. The ceremony will include speeches by esteemed guests, presentation of diplomas, and a celebration of the graduates' accomplishments.

The 6th convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of the year 2023 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community. It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, re-search, and professional development.

This year's convocation has selected the topic "The emerging data science and digital innovation ecosystem of Tanzania" as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 November African Statis- tics Day. The theme states "Modernising data ecosystem to accelarate the implemetation of African Continental Free Trade (AfCFTA): The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development in Tanzania.
